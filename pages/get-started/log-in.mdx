## Register user

Your organisation will register who should have access to Arealize. Contact [Benedicte](mailto:benedicte@arealize.ai) if you have not received user information.

## Log in

Navigate to [Dashboard](https://dashboard.arealize.ai/) and log in with e-mail and password.
