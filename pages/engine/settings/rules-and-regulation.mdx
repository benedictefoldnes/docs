---
title: Rules and regulation
description: 'Get alerts when the workplace safety law is violated'
---

Rules and regulations can be found in the right side menu, and is a feature that helps to ensure that the room solution is realistic and compliant with the workplace safety law and guidelines for universal design of office spaces.

<Frame type="glass">
    <img src="/images/engine/settings/rules-and-regulation/example.png" />
</Frame>

## Example situation

<Steps>
  <Step title="You receive an alert">
    When a potential conflict is detected, you will see a warning triangle in the area that is affected. Here we have added more desks than recommended.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/settings/rules-and-regulation/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read alert">
    Click the "Alert"-icon in the right side menu to see all potential conflicts in the floor.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/settings/rules-and-regulation/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take action">
    We recommend to take one of the suggested compensating actions. We see that the area is too small in relation to the number of desks. By removing some desks, the alert will disappear.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/settings/rules-and-regulation/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
