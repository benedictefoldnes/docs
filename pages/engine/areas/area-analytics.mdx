## General area overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open statistics panel">
    In the left sidebar, open the "Statistics" tab.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/areas/area-analytics/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Statistics overview">
    The statistics panel organises rooms into room types and shows the number of rooms and total area of each room type.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/areas/area-analytics/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inspect areas">
    Click on an area to get the area details.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/areas/area-analytics/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change area properties

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an area">
    Select an area type or a specific area by clicking on a row in the statistics panel. This will open up the properties panel for the selection.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/areas/area-analytics/step-4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change properties">
    Change room type, name, area type, etc. The changes will save automatically.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/areas/area-analytics/step-5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
