---
title: Create zone
description: 'Create zones to group areas'
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an area">
    Hold the "Shift"-button and click on the areas to select them.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/areas/create-zone/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create zone">
    <Info>
      Areas can be part of one or more zones.
    </Info>
    In the left menu, select the dropdown menu "Zone", and click "Create new zone" to create a new zone, or select an existing zone.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/areas/create-zone/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate zone">
    To isolate the statistics for your zone, go to the right sidepanel and open "Settings". Select your zone in the dropdown menu "Active zone" to isolate the area.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/areas/create-zone/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
