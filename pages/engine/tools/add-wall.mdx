## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select wall tool">
    From the toolbar at the top, select the "Wall" tool.
    <Tip>
      You can also use the keyboard shortcut "W" (Wall).
    </Tip>
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-wall/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place wall">
    Click once to start the wall, and click again to finish. You can also hold [Shift] to continue the wall.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-wall/step-2.gif" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure wall">
    Use the left sidebar to change the thickness of the wall.
    <Tip>
      Use the keyboard shortcut "R" to make the wall into a zone marker. The wall is now invisible, but the area is marked. Press "R" again to change back to a visible wall.
    </Tip>
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-wall/step-3.gif" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

Known issues and solutions.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete a door?">
    Click on a door, or hold [Shift] and click to select multiple doors. Then press [Delete] to delete.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
