## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select door tool">
    From the toolbar at the top, select the "Door" tool.
    <Tip>
      You can also use the keyboard shortcut "E" (Entry).
    </Tip>
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-door/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place door">
    Use the mouse to move the door to the correct position, and click to confirm placement.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-door/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure door">
    Use the left sidebar to flip the door in the desired direction. Set the width of the door and change the door type if desired.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-door/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

Known issues and solutions.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete a door?">
    Click on a door, or hold [Shift] and click to select multiple doors. Then press [Delete] to delete.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
