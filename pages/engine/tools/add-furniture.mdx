## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open furniture panel">
    From the right sidepanel, click on the 'Furniture'-icon.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select category">
    Furniture is organized in categories of room types. Select a room type that fits the room you want to furnish.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust furniture type">
    Some furniture can be adjusted in different dimensions and sizes. Use the dropdown menu to select the correct furniture type.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag furniture">
    To place the furniture, drag and drop it onto the floor.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/step-4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rotate furniture">
    You can rotate the furniture with 15-degree increments by dragging.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/step-5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Advanced usage

### Add site locations

Workstations can be quickly added by using the "+"-symbol in the direction you want to add.

<Frame type="glass">
    <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/advanced-1.png" />
</Frame>

### Duplicate furniture

You can duplicate single furniture and furniture groups by clicking the 'Copy'-icon for the selected furniture.

<Tip>
    You can also use the keyboard shortcut "Ctrl" + C (copy) and "Ctrl" + V (paste).
</Tip>

<Frame type="glass">
    <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/advanced-2.png" />
</Frame>

### Group furniture

Single furniture can be grouped together into a furniture group. To group multiple standalone furniture, select the furniture and click "Create group".

<Frame type="glass">
    <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/advanced-3.png" />
</Frame>

<Info>
    Furniture groups can be disassembled by selecting the group and clicking "Disassemble group".
</Info>

<Frame type="glass">
    <img src="/images/engine/tools/add-furniture/advanced-4.png" />
</Frame>

## Troubleshooting

Known issues and solutions.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete furniture?">
    Click on a piece of furniture, or hold [Shift] and click to select multiple pieces of furniture. Then press [Delete] to delete.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
