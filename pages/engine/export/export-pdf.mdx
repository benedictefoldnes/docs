## Advanced use

### Zone view

You can isolate a zone by opening the dropdown menu and selecting one or more zones. This hides all other areas.

<Frame type="glass">
    <img src="/images/engine/export/export-pdf/select-zone.png" />
</Frame>

### Add page

You can add multiple pages to your PDF file. Click the "Add page" button at the bottom of the document. This allows you to have multiple different views spread across multiple pages.

<Frame type="glass">
    <img src="/images/engine/export/export-pdf/add-sheet.png" />
</Frame>
