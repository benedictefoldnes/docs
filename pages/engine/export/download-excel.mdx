## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open statistics panel">
    In the toolbar to the right, click the Export menu and select "Download PDF".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/export/download-excel/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Download Excel'">
    In the toolbar to the right, click the Export menu and select "Download Excel".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/export/download-excel/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Downloading">
    The Excel file will be downloaded and saved to your downloads folder.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/export/download-excel/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
