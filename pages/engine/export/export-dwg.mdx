title: Export DWG
description: 'Export the floor to CAD-formatet DWG.'
---

DWG is the standard format for CAD drawings, developed by Autodesk. When you export to DWG, you get a 2D version of your floor that can be opened and edited in all known CAD programs such as Archicad, AutoCAD, Revit and Rhino.
