## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Duplicate floor">
    In the tool bar menu, select the hamburger-icon and click on "Duplicate".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/duplicate-floor/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change name">
    Write the desired name for the duplicate.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/engine/duplicate-floor/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Open duplicate

The duplicate is now under the floor in Dashboard and can be opened in the editing tool.

<Frame type="glass">
    <img src="/images/engine/duplicate-floor/my-draft.png" />
</Frame>
