---
title: Add tenant
description: 'How to create a tenant with custom requirements'
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'Tenants'">
    In the left sidebar, click on "Tenants".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/create-tenant/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New tenant'">
    To create a tenant, click on the button "New tenant".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/create-tenant/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Fill in tenant name and number of employees. You can choose to specify the area limits for the tenant by filling in minimum and maximum area.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/create-tenant/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit room list">
    Click "+" and "-" to add or remove number of rooms. To add a new room, you can choose "Add room".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/create-tenant/step-4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add room">
    Specify a room type, approximate size of the room, and optionally number of desks. Click "Confirm" to add the room.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/create-tenant/step-5a.png" />
    </Frame>
    <Tip>
        You can define a custom room type by choosing "Add 'Your room type'".
    </Tip>
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/create-tenant/step-5b.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add comment">
    If there are special needs that are not covered in the room list, you can write it in the comment field. Click "Confirm" to add the tenant requirements.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/create-tenant/step-6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
