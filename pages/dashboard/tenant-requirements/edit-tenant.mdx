## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit tenant">
    To edit the tenant's room plan, you can use the search field to find your tenant, and then click "Edit".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/edit-tenant/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make changes">
    You can change details about the tenant, edit the room list, and make changes in the comment field. Click "Confirm" to save the changes.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/tenant-requirements/edit-tenant/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
