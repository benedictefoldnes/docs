## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add building">
    Click "New building".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/new-building/add-building/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Fill in name, address and organisation number (optional).
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/new-building/add-building/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add category">
    Categories can help you organise buildings. Examples of categories are location name or responsible person. Finish with clicking "Create".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/new-building/add-building/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search building">
    You can use the search field to find and change or create floors for the building.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/new-building/add-building/step-4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
