---
title: Digitalise floor
icon: robot
description: 'How to digitalise a floor plan'
---

When you digitalise a floor, the floor plan becomes editable. You can make changes to areas and furniture, as well as export statistics and the floor plan in multiple formats.

<Frame type="ghost" caption="Example of an activated floor plan in the editing tool.">
    <img src="/images/dashboard/digitalise-floor/digitalisation.png" />
</Frame>

## Fremgangsm√•te

<Steps>
    <Step title="Activate floor">
        To create an editable version, you need to start an activation. Click "Activate floor".
        <Frame type="ghost">
            <img src="/images/dashboard/digitalise-floor/step-1.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select 'Digitalisation'">
        Select "Digitalise floor plan". Click "Next".
        <Frame type="ghost">
            <img src="/images/dashboard/digitalise-floor/step-2.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Upload file">
        Upload a PDF (or DWG) file of the floor plan, or select the correct file if you have already uploaded it.
        <Frame type="ghost">
            <img src="/images/dashboard/digitalise-floor/step-3.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Confirm digitalisation">
        Review the details and click "Confirm" to start digitalisation of the floor plan.
        <Frame type="ghost">
            <img src="/images/dashboard/digitalise-floor/step-4.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Get notified">
        You will receive an email when the floor plan is ready. Click the link to visit the floor.
        <Info>
            The process can take up to 24 hours.
        </Info>
        <Frame type="ghost">
            <img src="/images/dashboard/digitalise-floor/step-5.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Get notified">
        The floor plan is now ready. You can make changes to the floor plan, and export it in multiple formats.
        <Frame type="ghost">
            <img src="/images/dashboard/digitalise-floor/step-6.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>

It is recommended to turn on [Notifications](/pages/dashboard/settings/notifications). This will ensure you get notified when your floor plans are available.
