## Steps

You can organise your buildings using categories (tags). Categories can be reused on multiple buildings, and are useful for grouping multiple buildings in relation to responsible person ("Rolf") or location ("Bergen"). You can name categories freely.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Create category">
        Select "Create category" to begin.
        <Frame type="ghost">
            <img src="/images/dashboard/settings/create-category/step-1.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select category">
        Select one or more categories from the list, or add a new one. Click outside the menu to confirm the changes.
        <Frame type="ghost">
            <img src="/images/dashboard/settings/create-category/step-2.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>

Selected categories are shown in the list of buildings.

</Frame>
