## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add floor">
    Click the "Add floor" button.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/buildings/add-floors/step-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload floor plan">
    Write the floor number, for example "U1", "3", or "Roof". Drag and drop a PDF (or DWG) for the floor, and click "Next".
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/buildings/add-floors/step-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm upload">
    Review the details and click "Confirm" to confirm the upload.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/buildings/add-floors/step-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change details and upload additional files">
    The floor is under activation, but you can still make changes, or delete and add new files.
    <Frame type="glass">
        <img src="/images/dashboard/buildings/add-floors/step-4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
